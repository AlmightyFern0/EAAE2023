School of architecture(S)

Architecture as a discipline entangles multiple interactions between the physical world, technology and the organisation of the cultural and social environment of the profession. In the era of Postarchitecture moving towards an autonomous subjectivity, open up the field questioning the plurality of the discipline: not just architecture but architecture(s). Furthermore, European Schools for Architectural Education offer a wide variety of meanings of the term Architecture in their curricula. For all these reasons is urgent to discuss a broadening of the term itself to understand the positioning of Architectural education in the global contemporary world.


Antagonist characters polarise human knowledge. While the practice is moving towards an autonomous subjectivity, Architecture can be understood as dichotomies leading to a new paradigm beyond Postarchitecture: architecture(s). The conference is an invitation to think differently, reflecting upon the context of the discipline to understand the knowledge of the future focusing on the question: what is Architecture in the age often described as post Architecture?


In this frame this conference attempts to reason around a contemporary broad and inclusive definition of Architecture through the discussion of six antinomian couple of concepts: Architecture as a method and/or as a discipline, Architecture of the Masters and/or of the topics, Architecture for architects and/or for the community, Architecture as avant-garde and/or market-oriented, Architecture inside and/or outside the wall, Architecture disciplinary and/or extra-disciplinary. The six dichotomies will be grouped into the tree main sessions: Seeds of architecture: Ways of Teaching, Roots of Architecture: Ways of Research, Branches of Architecture: Ways of Practice.
