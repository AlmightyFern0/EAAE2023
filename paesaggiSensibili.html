Paesaggi Sensibili

Paesaggisensibili is an independent observatory on contemporary landscapes, a space for research and experimentation of new approaches for the understanding of the dynamics of our time through the engagement of the territories. We create territorial narratives that foster an interdisciplinary research on contemporary landscape.
